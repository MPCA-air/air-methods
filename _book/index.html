Updated Jun 06, 2017

Introduction

This guide describes the methods used at the MPCA to analyze air monitoring and modeling data. The charts and code found in this guide were produced using the freely available R statistical software. To follow along with the examples, you can download a copy of R to your computer from the r-project or open R-Fiddle in your browser.
